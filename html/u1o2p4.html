With a little more time...


Unit 1 - Outcome 2.4

Describe further improvements you can make to your project.

I’m somewhat of a perfectionist, and I don’t think I’d ever consider something I’ve made ‘perfect’. There’s always something else that I could have improved upon if only I’d had a little more time.

I’d say that the main thing I’d improve if I had more time would be to relocate the navigation panel either to the top of the page or to the bottom of the active window. Both of these are fully viable, but they’re beyond my current knowledge and they would take some time to research and understand.

I could always work upon the aesthetics of the site too, but I’m reasonably confident that there’s little I can do to be happier with the end result; I’m proud of what I’ve done and I see no reason to unleash my inner perfectionist with this project.
