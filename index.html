About me

I'm a 24 year-old professional programmer (in training). I've always wanted to create my own software from as early as I could understand the concept, and for as long as I can remember I've been tinkering with computers, trying to learn as much as I can out of pure curiosity. My life-long dream is to create a game independently, but creating software of any kind is my passion.

Some of my earliest memories are of creating basic webpages with notepad and tiny programs using batch. Since then, I've experimented with HTML, CSS, Javascript, Java, Actionscript, C++, Python, CSV, XML, JSON, Jquery, Swift, Visual Basic, Game Maker Language, Lua, Linux, Microsoft/Open/Libre Office, the Adobe suite and a fair few other technoligies. (That's just as many as I could think of at the time of writing this).

If you're interested in what games I like, I could include a similarly oversized list, but I'd much rather say what interests me about them. I love games which allow your imagination to explode; which let you delve into a world that is complex beyond understanding put propels you to try anyway. It doesn't matter whether the complexity comes from the game mechanics, the lore, character depth or even just the size of the game world. As long as the game has character, depth and/or intrigue I'll probably love it.

The first page will give you a similar description of myself to what you've already read here. Most of the information on this site is about the site itself and my progress in making it.
